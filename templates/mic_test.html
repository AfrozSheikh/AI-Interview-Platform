Microphone Test

Before starting the interview, let's test your microphone and speech recognition.

Browser Compatibility:

  • Best supported in Google Chrome
  • Also works in Microsoft Edge and Safari
  • Firefox requires manual enablement of Web Speech API

Step 1: Test Microphone

Click the button and speak to test your microphone.

Step 2: Test Speech Recognition

Your speech will appear here...

Speech recognition: Off

Speak clearly and at a normal pace.

Try saying: "Hello, this is a test of my microphone."

Troubleshooting

No microphone detected?

Check if your microphone is properly connected and selected as the default input device.

Permission denied?

Click the lock icon in your browser's address bar and allow microphone access.

Speech not recognized?

Try using Chrome browser. Ensure you're in a quiet environment and speaking clearly.
